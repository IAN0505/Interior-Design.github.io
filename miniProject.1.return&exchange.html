Placing Your Order

You will have a chance to review and verify your order during checkout. When everything has been reviewed, you can proceed to “Place Your Order.” Within approximately 1 to 2 business days, you will receive an email confirmation that your order has been received.

Placing An Order By Phone

If you would like to place an order by phone, please call one of our associates at (918) 561.6345 between the hours of 10:00 am and 6:00 pm EST (M-S).

Returns


Stock Products

Stock products can be returned to us in their original packaging with a receipt up to 7 days after receiving your order. Return shipping charges will not be refunded, unless the return is due to a manufacturing defect or shipping damage. All products are subject to a restocking fee of 25%. All items require an Return Authorization Number (RA#). Please contact customer service for an RA#. Items without an RA# will be refused.

Custom Products

Custom items that are made to order are nonreturnable. We will repair or exchange any custom order with a manufacturing defect or shipping damage. Whether the order is repaired or exchanged is at The Refuge Lifestyle’s sole discretion.

Shipping


Standard Shipping and Processing (In-stock Small Good Items)

Most purchases are shipped directly from our suppliers within 2 to 3 weeks of receipt of the order. If there is a delay, we will notify you by mail or email. Please allow 2 to 3 weeks for delivery. These items are drop shipped and some may require a signature. If a signature is required, the delivery company will notify you. Items ordered together may not arrive in the same box or at the same time.
